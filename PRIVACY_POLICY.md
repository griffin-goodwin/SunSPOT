# SunSPOT Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

## Overview

SunSPOT ("the App") is a space weather monitoring application that displays publicly available solar and geomagnetic data. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles information.

## Data Collection

**SunSPOT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App:
- Does NOT require user accounts or login
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your location
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- Does NOT contain advertisements
- Does NOT share any data with third parties

## Data Sources

The App displays publicly available space weather data from:
- **NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC)** - Solar flare alerts, geomagnetic storm warnings, and space weather forecasts
- **Helioviewer.org** - Solar imagery from NASA's Solar Dynamics Observatory (SDO)

This data is fetched directly from public APIs and is not stored permanently on your device beyond temporary caching for performance.

## Local Storage

The App may store the following locally on your device:
- User preferences (notification settings, display preferences)
- Temporary cached data for offline viewing

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App will send local alerts about space weather events. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device. You can disable notifications at any time through the App settings or your device's Settings app.

## Children's Privacy

SunSPOT does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general audiences interested in space weather.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
